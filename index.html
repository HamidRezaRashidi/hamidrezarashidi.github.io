How it works

A predictable process from napkin sketch to sealed drawings.

Step 01

Discovery & site review

Share your plans, photos, and goals. We review the site, loads, and local jurisdiction requirements before quoting the scope.

  • Free 20-minute consultation
  • Fixed-fee proposal within 48 hours
  • Clear scope, deliverables, and timeline
Step 02

Analysis & design

Our engineers model the structure, run gravity and lateral analyses, and optimize members for cost, constructability, and performance.

  • IBC / IRC / ASCE 7 compliant
  • Steel, wood, concrete, and masonry
  • Coordinated with architects and MEP teams
Step 03

Sealed drawings & support

Receive stamped plans, calculations, and details — plus responsive support through permitting, bidding, and construction.

  • Sealed PDF + DWG deliverables
  • Plan-check response turnaround in 3–5 days
  • Optional site observation & RFIs

FAQ

Frequently asked questions.

What states are you licensed in?

Our PEs are licensed across 12 states, primarily in the Midwest and Northeast. Tell us your project location and we'll confirm coverage within a day.

How quickly can you turn around a project?

Small residential projects are typically delivered in 1–2 weeks. Larger commercial jobs are scoped during the free consultation.

Do you provide sealed drawings?

Yes. Every set of structural drawings and calculations is signed and sealed by a licensed PE in the project's jurisdiction.

Can you work with my architect or contractor?

Absolutely. We coordinate directly with architects, contractors, and MEP engineers to keep the design and schedule aligned.

Do you offer free consultations?

Yes — every project starts with a free 20-minute consultation to scope the work and confirm we're the right fit.
